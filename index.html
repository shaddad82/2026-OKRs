Status Guide & Scoring Reference

Reference criteria for assigning OKR status values

Category On Track 🟢 At Risk 🟡 Off Track 🔴 Not Started / N/A
Project (Overall) On time and on budget; all major milestones on track 2+ milestones at risk; likelihood of timeline/budget impact if not mitigated 1+ major milestones missed with no risk mitigation plan; timeline and budget at risk Not applicable
Objectives & Team Alignment Key objectives defined; stakeholders engaged and agree to assigned tasks Objectives defined but stakeholders are neutral or don't fully agree Objectives not defined and/or stakeholders do not agree or participate Not applicable
Resources Adequately staffed; team dedicated to completing assigned tasks Understaffed and/or team lacks full capacity, creating late task patterns Understaffed; no capacity — adverse impact on schedule, quality, and/or budget Not applicable
Schedule On schedule Behind schedule; critical path not impacted Behind schedule; critical path adversely impacted Not applicable
Cost On budget Mitigation plan may cause budget overage No mitigation plan or plan will cause budget overage Not applicable
Benefit Key benefits defined, data identified, formula approved, report requirements designed & implemented prior to go-live Plan to identify & measure benefits is partial; may not be ready before go-live No key measurements in place and no risk mitigation plan Not applicable
📝 Scoring Notes
  • All criteria "On Track" → overall status = On Track
  • Only one criteria "At Risk" → overall status = On Track
  • Two or more criteria "At Risk" → overall status = At Risk
  • Any criteria "Off Track" → overall status = Off Track
  • If "Off Track", status remains Off Track until a resolution is defined and agreed upon. Only then can status move back to On Track.
  • Notes should explain why an item is not On Track and how it can recover.
  • Every column should be filled out. Date columns should have actual dates, not quarter/month estimates.
🔢 Score Values
Complete = 100% On Track = 75% At Risk = 50% Off Track = 25% Not Started = 0%
